How Solar Energy Works

Sunshine power! Learn how we catch sunlight and turn it into electricity.

Ages 5 – 7

Power From the Sun!

The sun gives us light and warmth every day. But did you know we can also use sunshine to make electricity? Let’s find out how!

Our Amazing Sun

So warm!

The sun is a giant ball of fire in space. It sends light and heat to Earth every single day. Without the sun, there would be no life on our planet!

Sunlight is full of energy. In just one hour, the sun sends enough energy to Earth to power the whole world for a whole year!

☀️ Fun fact: The sun is so far away that its light takes 8 minutes to reach us — and it travels at 186,000 miles per second!

How Do Solar Panels Work?

⚡ Electricity!

Solar panels are made of special material called silicon. When sunlight hits the panel, tiny bits of light called photons knock little things called electrons loose.

Those moving electrons create electricity! Think of it like a waterslide — the sun pushes the electrons down the slide, and that motion is electricity.

No smoke, no noise, no pollution. Just clean energy from sunshine!

💡 Fun fact: Solar panels have no moving parts! They sit still and quietly turn sunlight into electricity — no spinning needed.

Solar Panels Are Everywhere!

You can put solar panels on the roof of a house, a school, or even a barn! They sit up high where they can catch the most sunshine.

Big solar farms have thousands of panels covering huge fields. They make enough electricity for whole towns!

Some solar panels even follow the sun across the sky, like a sunflower turning its face!

🏠 Fun fact: A house with solar panels on the roof can sometimes make MORE electricity than it needs — and share the extra with neighbors!

What Happens at Night?

Zzz
No sun? Panels rest!

When the sun goes down, solar panels stop making electricity. They need sunlight to work — moonlight isn’t strong enough!

That’s why solar energy needs friends. We can store sunshine energy in batteries during the day and use it at night. Or use wind turbines that work when it’s dark!

Clean energy is a team sport!

🔋 Key idea: Solar energy is free, quiet, and clean. The sun will keep shining for billions more years — that’s a LOT of free electricity!
