Dinosaurs & Fossil Fuels

A long, long time ago… and why it matters today!

Ages 5 – 7

What Do Dinosaurs Have to Do with Energy?

A very, very long time ago, dinosaurs walked the Earth. Let’s go on an adventure to find out what happened to them — and why it matters for our sky today!

Meet the Dinosaurs!

Millions and millions of years ago, big dinosaurs walked around everywhere! There were also giant trees, huge ferns, and tiny sea creatures.

The Earth was very different back then — warm, green, and full of life.

🦖 Fun fact: Some dinosaurs were as tall as a 4-story building! Others were as small as a chicken.

What Happened to Them?

Ground level
Millions of years ↓

When the dinosaurs and old plants died, they got buried deep, deep underground. Dirt and rocks piled on top of them.

After millions and millions of years, all that squishing and heat turned them into something new: coal, oil, and gas.

We call these “fossil fuels” because they come from old fossils!

🧬 Fun fact: The oil and coal we use today took hundreds of millions of years to form. That’s way before people were even around!

People Burn It — And That Makes Smoke

People found the coal, oil, and gas underground. They started burning it to make electricity, drive cars, and heat houses.

But when you burn fossil fuels, it makes dirty smoke that goes up into the sky. You can’t always see it, but it’s there!

🚘 Fun fact: A regular car puts out about 4 tons of invisible gas into the air every year. That’s as heavy as an elephant!

The Invisible Blanket

Heat gets trapped!

All that invisible smoke goes up and makes a blanket around the Earth.

When the sun shines on us, some of the heat is supposed to bounce back into space. But the blanket traps the heat and keeps it in. That makes the Earth get warmer and warmer.

It’s like wearing a big coat on a hot day — you get way too warm!

🌡️ Fun fact: This invisible gas is called “carbon dioxide” or CO₂ for short. Plants breathe it in, but we’re making way too much of it!

Earth Is Getting Too Warm

When Earth gets too warm, it causes problems:

Ice melts — the ice at the North and South Poles is melting. Polar bears and penguins are losing their homes.

Weather goes wild — bigger storms, hotter summers, and strange weather.

Animals need help — some animals can’t find food or cool places to live.

🠣 Fun fact: Polar bears need sea ice to hunt for food. When the ice melts, they have to swim farther and farther. We can help by using less fossil fuel!

We Can Fix This!

Here’s the good news: we don’t have to keep burning old dinosaur fuel!

We can use the sun, the wind, and water to make clean energy instead. No dirty smoke. No invisible blanket. No more making Earth too hot.

Every solar panel and wind turbine helps the Earth breathe easier!

🌎 Key idea: Fossil fuels are old, dirty, and make our planet too warm. Clean energy from the sun, wind, and water keeps our air clean and our Earth happy!
